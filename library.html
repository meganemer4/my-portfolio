Neo-Gothic Library

I designed and built this German castle library environment that has a 19th century neo-gothic style. For this project, I focused on creating heavily detailed models and textures.

References

I used the interiors of these German and Romanian castles for inspiration. These castles were all either built or re-designed in the 19th century.

From left to right: Drachenburg, Schwerin, Peles, Neuschwanstein, and Hohenzollern.


Concept

This is my concept drawing of the environment. The Neo-Gothic style is very organic, and I incorporated it into the design using references from the castles above.

Texturing

I hand-painted some of the textures in Procreate, including the map, duchess portrait, and rug design, and textured most of the other assets in Substance Painter. I also made a customizable wood grain material in Substance Designer that I incorporated into a lot of the textures.




I also wrote a short mel script to randomly assign the book materials to the books, pictured below.
