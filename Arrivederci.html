WORK REEL ABOUT RESUME

Arrivederci is a 3D animated short of a pop-up book, which explores four different locations in Italy. It illustrates the story of a girl who traveled all around Italy and created a detailed pop-up book after coming home. Arrivederci, which translates to “until we meet again,” showcases her memories in Venice, Bari, San Gimignano, and Milan, four cities that represent four of the diverse regions of Italy.
