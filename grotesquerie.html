WORK REEL ABOUT RESUME

Grotesquerie Teaser

This is a teaser for FX Network's "Grotesquerie", which I worked on while at the Mill. For this project, I did the lighting and rendering for several smoke element shots and the close-up human eyeball shot, which I lit to match the background plates. Below are stills of each of the shots I worked on.


These shots were rendered using Houdini and Arnold. The smoke shots were particularly fun to work on because I was matching the smoke to such stylized plates with an interesting color pallete.




For the eye shot below, I matched the lighting on the eyeball to match up with the one in the plate.
